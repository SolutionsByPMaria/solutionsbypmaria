📥 How to Download

Our digital products are delivered instantly — no shipping, no waiting. Here's everything you need to know about accessing your purchase quickly and easily.

How do I receive my digital product after purchase? +
After your purchase is confirmed on Payhip or Gumroad, you will receive an automatic email containing your secure download link. Simply click the link in the email, and your file(s) will begin downloading immediately. Check your spam or junk folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes of purchase.
How long do I have to download my purchase? +
Your download link is available for an extended period after purchase — typically 30 days or more depending on the platform. We recommend downloading your files immediately after purchase and saving them to a secure location on your computer or cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) so you always have access.
What file formats will I receive? +
The formats included vary by product and are clearly listed on each product listing page. Common formats include: PDF (ready-to-print, non-editable), Microsoft Word (.docx, fully editable), Microsoft Excel (.xlsx, for trackers and forms), and Canva (editable via free Canva account using an included template link). Many products include multiple formats in one download.
I didn't receive my download email — what should I do? +
First, check your spam, junk, or promotions folder — automated emails sometimes land there. If you still don't see it, log into your Payhip or Gumroad account directly and navigate to your purchase history — your download will be available there. If you're still having trouble, contact us at solutions@pbymaria.com with your order details and we'll resolve it promptly.
Can I download my purchase on my phone or tablet? +
Yes! You can download and open most of our products on a smartphone or tablet. PDF files open in any PDF viewer app. Word files open in Microsoft Word or Google Docs mobile apps. Canva templates can be accessed and edited directly through the free Canva mobile app. For the best editing experience, we recommend a desktop or laptop computer.
What if my download link has expired or is not working? +
If your download link has expired or isn't working, simply contact us at solutions@pbymaria.com with your name, email address used for purchase, and order confirmation number. We will verify your purchase and send you a fresh download link as quickly as possible — usually within 1 business day.

✏️ Customization

Our templates are designed to be personalized — put your name, logo, and brand on them in minutes. Here's how to make every product uniquely yours.

Can I edit and customize the templates after I download them? +
Yes! Most of our products are fully editable so you can personalize them with your business name, logo, contact information, colors, and more. Editable formats include Microsoft Word (.docx), Microsoft Excel (.xlsx), and Canva. PDF-only products are designed as ready-to-use, print-and-go documents and cannot be edited. Each listing clearly states which formats are included.
Do I need any special software to customize the templates? +
For Word templates: Microsoft Word (PC or Mac) or Google Docs (free). For Excel templates: Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets (free). For Canva templates: a free Canva account at canva.com — no paid subscription required for our templates. For PDF files: no editing is needed — simply print or fill in by hand or using PDF form fields if included.
How do I access and edit a Canva template? +
After downloading your product, you will find a link inside the file or in your confirmation email that says "Access Canva Template" or similar. Click that link — it will open the template directly in Canva (you'll need a free account). Click "Use Template" to create your own editable copy. From there, simply click any text, image, or element to customize it. Your changes are saved automatically to your Canva account.
Can I add my own logo and brand colors to the templates? +
Absolutely! All editable templates are designed with customization in mind. In Word: replace placeholder text and insert your logo via Insert > Pictures. In Canva: click any element to edit text, change colors to match your brand palette, and upload your logo directly to the design. We recommend customizing every template with your business name, logo, and contact information before sharing with clients.
Can I translate the templates into another language? +
Our templates are created in English, but you are welcome to translate editable versions (Word or Canva) for your own personal or business use. Simply edit the text fields and replace the English content with your preferred language. Please note that translated versions are still subject to our standard licensing terms and may not be resold or redistributed.
What if a template doesn't look right or has a formatting issue after I edit it? +
Formatting issues can occasionally occur when opening Word files on different versions of Microsoft Office or Google Docs. If you experience this, try: (1) opening the file in Microsoft Word instead of Google Docs, (2) using the original downloaded file rather than a copy, or (3) contacting us with a screenshot of the issue. We will do our best to assist and send a corrected version if needed.

💳 Refunds & Policies

We stand behind the quality of every product we create. Please read our policies carefully — because digital files are delivered instantly, our refund policy is designed to be fair to both parties.

Do you offer refunds on digital products? +
Due to the instant and permanent nature of digital downloads, all sales are final and we do not offer refunds or exchanges once a product has been downloaded or accessed. We encourage you to carefully review all product descriptions, format details, preview images, and our FAQ before purchasing. What if I accidentally purchased the wrong product? +
If you purchased the wrong product and have not yet downloaded or accessed the file, contact us at solutions@pbymaria.com within 24 hours of purchase with your order details. We will review on a case-by-case basis and may be able to issue a store credit or exchange for the correct product. Requests submitted after the file has been downloaded cannot be honored.
What if my file is corrupted, defective, or not working? +
If you receive a file that is corrupted, incomplete, or technically defective — that is 100% our responsibility to fix. Contact us within 48 hours of your purchase at solutions@pbymaria.com with your order number and a description of the issue. We will send you a replacement file promptly at no additional charge. Technical issues caused by incompatible software on the buyer's end do not qualify as defective files.
My product didn't meet my expectations — can I get a refund? +
We put tremendous care into every product we create and provide detailed descriptions and preview images for every listing. Because digital products cannot be "returned," we are unable to offer refunds based on subjective dissatisfaction. However, if you feel a product was materially misrepresented in its listing, please reach out — we take feedback seriously and will review your concern individually.
Can I get a refund if I no longer need the product? +
Unfortunately, change-of-mind refunds are not available for digital products. Once a digital file has been purchased and the download link delivered, the transaction is considered complete. We recommend reviewing all product details, previews, and this FAQ thoroughly before completing your purchase to ensure it meets your needs.
How do I contact you about a purchase issue? +
For any issue related to a purchase — technical problems, missing downloads, billing questions, or other concerns — email us at solutions@pbymaria.com. Please include: your full name, the email address used for purchase, your order or transaction number, and a description of the issue. We respond to all purchase-related inquiries within 1–2 business days and will do our best to make every experience a positive one.

Understanding how you can and cannot use our products protects both you and our business. Here's a clear breakdown of your rights as a buyer.

What license do I receive when I purchase a digital product? +
When you purchase a product from Solutions by P Maria, you receive a non-exclusive, non-transferable personal and single-business use license. This means you may use the template within your own business — including with your own clients — but you may not sell, share, gift, redistribute, or claim authorship of the original template design. The license covers one business entity per purchase.
Can I use the templates with my own clients? +
Yes! You are fully licensed to use our templates within your own business and with your clients. For example, if you are a virtual assistant, you may use our Client Welcome Packet template when onboarding your clients. If you are a notary, you may use our Intake Form with your clients. What you may not do is resell or redistribute the template itself as a standalone product to others.
Can I resell or redistribute the templates I purchase? +
No. Reselling, redistributing, sharing, uploading, or sublicensing any of our templates — whether modified or unmodified — is strictly prohibited under our license agreement. Each template is sold for use by a single buyer in their own business. If you are interested in a commercial or multi-seat license for team or organizational use, contact us to discuss custom licensing options.

You may add your branding (logo, name, contact info, colors) to templates for use in your own business — but you may not claim to be the original creator or designer of the template, sell it as your own digital product, or present it as an original work you created. The underlying design, structure, and intellectual property remain the property of Solutions by P Maria.
Are there commercial licenses available for teams or agencies? +
Yes! If you represent an agency, team, organization, or training program that needs to use our templates across multiple people or businesses, we offer custom commercial licensing arrangements. Please contact us at solutions@pbymaria.com with details about your intended use, the number of users, and the products you're interested in. We will provide a custom quote and licensing agreement.
What happens if someone violates the license terms? +
Unauthorized use, resale, or redistribution of our products is a violation of our license agreement and may constitute copyright infringement under U.S. intellectual property law. We actively monitor for unauthorized listings of our products on resale platforms. Violations may result in a formal copyright infringement notice (DMCA takedown), legal action, and/or permanent account bans from our sales platforms. We ask that all buyers respect the time and expertise that goes into every product we create.

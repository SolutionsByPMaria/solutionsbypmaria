📋 Document Preparation

Our document preparation service helps individuals and businesses organize, complete, and present essential documents with accuracy and professionalism. Below are the most common questions about our process, timeline, pricing, and how we protect your information.

Standard document preparation is typically completed within 2–5 business days from the time we receive all required information and materials from you. More complex documents — such as multi-part estate organizers or business document packages — may take up to 7 business days. Upon consultation, we will give you an accurate estimated completion date based on your specific request. We do not begin work until all necessary details are provided.
Yes, rush services may be available depending on current workload and the complexity of your document. Rush requests are typically fulfilled within 24–48 hours and are subject to an additional expedited service fee. To request rush service, contact us as early as possible and clearly indicate your deadline. We will confirm availability and provide a custom quote before proceeding.
Your privacy is our highest priority. All information you share with Solutions by P Maria — including your name, address, financial details, family information, and any supporting documents — is kept strictly confidential. We do not sell, share, disclose, or transmit your personal information to any third party under any circumstances. All files are handled securely and deleted from our systems after a defined retention period or upon your request.
Absolutely. Upon request, we are happy to provide and sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before beginning any document preparation work. We take client trust seriously and welcome the opportunity to formalize our commitment to confidentiality in writing. Simply request an NDA during your initial consultation and we will prepare one promptly.
Our document preparation pricing is transparent and based on the type, length, and complexity of the document. We offer flat-rate pricing for most standard documents so you always know what to expect — no surprise charges. Simple single-page documents begin at a low flat rate, while multi-part document packages are priced accordingly. Contact us with your specific needs and we will provide a detailed, no-obligation quote before any work begins.
No — we believe in complete pricing transparency. The quote we provide at the start of your project is the price you pay. There are no hidden setup fees, revision charges (for standard edits), or surprise costs. Any additional fees — such as rush service charges or notarization fees if needed — will be clearly communicated and agreed upon before work begins. Your satisfaction and trust are more important to us than any extra charge.

Our administrative support services are designed to help busy professionals and small business owners stay organized, operate professionally, and reclaim their time. Here are answers to the most common questions about how we work, what we offer, and how to get started.

Yes! The majority of our administrative support services are available 100% virtually, making it easy to work with us no matter where you are located. We use secure, professional communication tools and file-sharing platforms to deliver work remotely with the same quality and attention to detail as in-person service. Virtual clients enjoy the same prompt response times, professional results, and personal touch as local clients.
Our virtual administrative support services include: document drafting and formatting, business correspondence writing, data entry and database management, email template creation, SOP (Standard Operating Procedure) development, client onboarding packet creation, appointment coordination support, general office organization, and digital file management. If you have a specific administrative need not listed here, contact us — chances are we can help.

Step 3: We provide a clear service proposal with pricing and timeline. Step 4: Once you approve, we get to work immediately. Most new clients are fully onboarded and receiving support within 3–5 business days of their initial inquiry.
To make our consultation as productive as possible, it helps to have a general idea of: the types of tasks or documents you need help with, how frequently you need support (one-time project vs. ongoing), any deadlines or time-sensitive needs, and your preferred method of communication (email, phone, video call). For time-sensitive requests received outside of business hours, we will respond first thing the following business day.

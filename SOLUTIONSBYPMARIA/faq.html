Notary Services

What is a notary public and what do you do?

A notary public is a state-commissioned official authorized to witness the signing of important documents, administer oaths, and verify identities to help prevent fraud. As a certified notary in Oklahoma, Princess Maria provides professional, accurate, and reliable notarization services for a wide range of personal and business documents.

What types of documents can you notarize?

I can notarize a wide variety of documents including affidavits, powers of attorney, real estate documents, loan signing packages, wills, trusts, medical consent forms, vehicle title transfers, and many more. If you're unsure whether your document qualifies, feel free to reach out and I'll let you know!

Do you offer mobile notary services?

Yes! As a mobile notary, I come to you — whether that's your home, office, hospital, or any other convenient location in the Spencer, Oklahoma area. Travel fees may apply depending on distance. Contact us to schedule your appointment.

How much does a notarization cost?

Notary fees in Oklahoma are regulated by state law. Standard notarization fees begin at the state-set rate per signature. Mobile notary services include a travel fee based on distance. Contact us for an exact quote based on your needs.

What do I need to bring to my notary appointment?

Please bring:

  • A valid, government-issued photo ID such as a driver's license or passport
  • The unsigned document(s) — documents should NOT be signed before the appointment
  • Any additional signers who are required to be present

All signers must appear in person.

Are you a loan signing agent?

Yes! In addition to general notary services, I am trained as a loan signing agent, helping facilitate the signing of mortgage and refinance loan packages. I work with title companies, escrow officers, and signing services to ensure smooth, accurate, and professional closings.

Document Preparation

What is document preparation and how is it different from legal advice?

Document preparation is the professional assistance with completing, organizing, and preparing legal-adjacent documents such as affidavits, powers of attorney, and estate organizers. Important: Document preparation is NOT legal advice. Princess Maria is not an attorney and does not provide legal counsel. For legal advice, please consult a licensed attorney.

What documents can you help me prepare?

We assist with preparing and organizing a variety of documents including:

  • General powers of attorney
